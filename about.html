About R.K. Enterprise

R.K. Enterprise is a professionally managed organization engaged in the manufacturing and supply of Container Houses, Porta Cabins, Portable Offices, Security Cabins, and Custom Container Structures.

We focus on delivering durable, cost-effective, and customizable modular solutions suitable for residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural applications.

Our commitment lies in quality craftsmanship, timely delivery, transparent pricing, and customer satisfaction.
