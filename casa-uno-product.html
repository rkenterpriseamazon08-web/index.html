About the Casa UNO Model

The Casa UNO Model is a sleek, compact, and highly efficient container home designed for modern living, site offices, and remote locations.

  • Container Size: 20 ft / 40 ft
  • Structure: Single Container
  • Usage: Home, Office, Studio
  • Insulation: PUF / Rockwool
  • Delivery: Pan-India

DETAILS

Exterior

  • 20FT “One Tripper” Shipping Container
  • All metalwork & structural steel reinforcement
  • Durable exterior-grade paint finish
  • 6' x 6'-8" vinyl sliding entry door with lock & screen
  • 3' x 3' vinyl sliding windows (double pane, Low-E)
  • 2 exterior lights near main entry

Walls, Ceilings & Doors

  • All structural framing completed
  • Finished drywall throughout
  • Sliding wood barn door at bathroom
  • LED lighting installed in all ceilings

Flooring

  • Luxury vinyl flooring
  • Multiple finish options available

Open Concept Kitchenette, Living Space & Bedroom

  • Quality cabinetry with soft-close hardware and upgraded box construction, topped with countertops and premium cabinet pulls
  • Floating butcher block shelves above countertops
  • Kitchen sink & faucet with pull-down sprayer
  • 2-burner electric induction cooktop (freestanding)
  • Dedicated space for a mini refrigerator under countertops
  • Flexible living / bedroom space suitable for couch or bed, coffee table, wall-mounted TV, or upgraded bunk bed system

Enclosed Bathroom

  • 32" × 32" shower with sliding glass door and luxury shower head
  • Ceramic vessel sink with modern faucet
  • Standard residential toilet with open shelving above
  • Integrated ventilation fan

*All container homes are built to IBC / IRC code standards. Adjustments can be made to meet state-specific regulations as required.*

Insulation

  • Insulated floor system for thermal efficiency
  • Spray foam insulation applied to walls and ceiling

HVAC

  • One 9,000 BTU, 19 SEER, 230V ductless mini-split air conditioner with integrated heat pump

Plumbing / Water

  • PEX piping and PVC drain systems with blackwater and greywater drains designed for connection to RV hookup or sanitary sewer systems
  • Connection to main water supply
  • 11kW electric tankless water heater

Electrical

  • External electrical hookups with full internal electrical distribution
  • Electrical outlets and LED lighting included throughout the container
  • All interior spaces feature professionally installed electrical systems meeting residential standards

20ft Rooftop Deck

  • Maintenance-free composite decking with treated framing
  • Aluminum railing system with gate and steel spiral staircase
  • Majority of construction completed by our team; minimal on-site assembly required due to shipping regulations

20ft Rooftop Deck Brackets

  • Welded rooftop deck brackets installed directly onto the container
  • Allows future installation of a rooftop deck using materials of your choice
  • Ideal option for customers planning a DIY or later-stage deck upgrade

Partial Exterior Cladding

  • Exterior cladding added to accent the container home design
  • Covers approximately 50% of the front exterior façade

Custom Camo Paint

  • Custom camouflage paint styles available to match your preferred theme

Interior Wall Upgrades

  • White shiplap
  • Pine, Amish pine, basswood, and Amish blue pine options available

Custom Built-In Bunk Beds

  • Custom-built bunk bed systems designed to maximize sleeping space
  • Ideal for offices, guest use, or multi-purpose living spaces

Deer Extension Pole System
