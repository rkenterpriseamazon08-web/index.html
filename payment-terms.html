Payment Terms

This Payment Terms policy outlines the payment structure and conditions applicable to container houses, site offices, and modular container solutions provided by R.K. Enterprise.

Advance Payment

A booking advance is mandatory to confirm any order. The advance amount is non-refundable once project execution begins.

Milestone-Based Payments

Payments are generally collected in stages such as order confirmation, fabrication progress, and dispatch or delivery.

Accepted Payment Methods

We accept payments via bank transfer, NEFT/RTGS, UPI, and other approved digital payment methods.

Payment Due Dates

All payments must be cleared as per agreed timelines. Delays in payment may result in project hold or rescheduling.

Taxes & Charges

All prices are exclusive of applicable taxes unless stated otherwise. GST and other statutory charges shall be levied as per government norms.

Late Payment Policy

Late payments may attract additional charges or interest as per the terms mentioned in the quotation or agreement.

Contact Information

For payment-related queries or clarifications, please contact our accounts or support team.
